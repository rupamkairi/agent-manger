# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Technical builders—developers and technical leads—who manage local AI agents and project resources while working across multiple projects.

## Product Purpose

Weave is a control panel for discovering, inspecting, configuring, and maintaining AI agents and their resources, including skills, instructions, memory, configs, and health. Success means helping builders understand system state quickly, make changes with confidence, and manage projects without unnecessary context switching. The most important outcome may shift between situational awareness, safe configuration, and operational reliability as the product develops.

## Positioning

Weave is a precise, calm control plane that gives technical builders one place to understand and maintain AI tooling across projects.

## Brand Personality

Calm polished utility: precise, restrained, quietly premium, technically confident, trustworthy, and clear.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic enterprise back-office dashboards, bureaucratic admin patterns, and oversized KPI-first layouts. Avoid dark hacker-console styling, neon theatrics, terminal cosplay, and cryptic interfaces. Useful AI-product conventions remain welcome when they serve the workflow rather than becoming decoration or dictating the information architecture.

## Design Principles

- Make system state legible at a glance.
- Optimize for safe, reversible work.
- Keep density purposeful and familiar.
- Reduce context switching across projects and resources.
- Earn trust through clear validation, conflict, error, loading, and success feedback.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use accessible defaults, semantic controls, visible focus states, adequate contrast, keyboard support, and reduced-motion support. Formal WCAG conformance is not a release gate, but accessibility should remain part of the normal product quality baseline.
